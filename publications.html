Evidence-Grounded Multimodal Misinformation Detection with Attention-Based GNNs

Sharad Duwal, Mir Nafis Sharear Shopnil, Abhishek Tyagi, Adiba Mahbub Proma

arXiv:2505.18221, Under review at ACL ARR (May 2025)

[arXiv]

Formalized evidence and claim graph representations with cross-graph attention GNN for image-text consistency scoring. Achieved 93.05% accuracy on evaluation set, outperforming best LLM baseline by 2.82 percentage points.
