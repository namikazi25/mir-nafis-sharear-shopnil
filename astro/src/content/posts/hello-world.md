This is where I'm going to think out loud about research — LLM reasoning, mechanistic interpretability, agentic systems. Less polished than a paper, more permanent than a tweet.

Three kinds of posts I want to write:

1. **Paper notes** — plain-English tours of work I'm involved in, with the messy behind-the-scenes context that never makes it into the PDF.
2. **Speculative essays** — half-formed research ideas I'd like someone to yell at me about.
3. **Field notes** — things I learn while shipping agents to production that I think are under-discussed in academia.

If you want email pings when I post, the [RSS feed](/rss.xml) is the honest way to do it. I'll probably never have a mailing list.

— M.
